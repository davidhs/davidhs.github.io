# Realtime Construction of Objects

I was watching Michael Stevens YouTube channel, in particular [this YouTube Short](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/_Nr4mvdkEVw), where he was showing a stuffed toy whose shape was of the rabbit-duck optical illusion, so depending on how you hold it or orient it your visual system prefers to see the object rabbit or the object duck.

To me this suggests an object you see, or the boundary of it, are "artificial" and constructed "after-the-fact" as some sort of overlay.
