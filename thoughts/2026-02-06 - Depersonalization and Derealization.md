# Depersonalization and Derealization

I recently realized that the movie **The Truman Show (1998)** by Peter Weir does a pretty good just at depicting _derealization_ in an artistic way, in the way when the world stops feeling real and feels fake.

A movie that I think probably does a decent job at depicting _depersonalization_ in an artistic way is **Get Out (2017)** by Jordan Peele, in particular the sunken place. This is where you feel distant and remote from your body, the sense of it, and may feel like it's as if you are watching a movie (distant/dissociated).
