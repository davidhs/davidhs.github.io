# Phantasia Exercise: There and Back Again

This is a phantasia exercise for those with hypophantasia. Its purpose is to make it more clear to you what is happening presently vs. being in an imagined space.

Say you are in the shower, look at something in the room, and now look at something in imagination. Maybe you are imagining what you need to do later, e.g. go to the store. Imagine yourself at the entrance of the store. Now direct your attention back to what is present, i.e. you are standing in the shower, then back to imagining the entrance to the store.

Do this back and forth, again and again, and try to develop a clear sense of what it feels to toggle between what is present and what is imagined. What changes? What does it feel like? Etc.
