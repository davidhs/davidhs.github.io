# Dream journal and dream-interruption induced visualization

I am (still) a hypophant, meaning my visualization ability/capacity is poor, but not as poor as aphantasia (completely lacking).

This morning when I was awoken unusually early, I was dreaming. When I woke up the memory of the dream was still very vivid, including visual detail and colors. As the time passes it becomes less and less vivid and I can no longer visualize the color, even though I know the color, but I remember for some moments after waking up I could still visualize the colors.

My guess then is that keeping a dream journal and perhaps even waking yourself in the middle of REM sleep (not a good idea in general) can help you with visualization, e.g. maybe it can help you find the mental moves or pathways that induce visualization.
